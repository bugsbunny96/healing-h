Therapy

Electrotherapy

Laser therapy, Diathermy, IFT, TENS, wax therapy, Ultrasonic massage etc.

Exercise Therapy

Hand Rehabilitation table, Continuous passive motion (CPM), stretching and strengthening exercises the whole body, pulley, shoulder wheel, cycle, twister etc.

Manual Therapy

Manual therapy is a skilled passive movement of hands mainly used to decrease pain, to enhance the mobility of nerves, joints, and soft tissues. This therapy employs a person’s hands instead of machines to heal the soft tissue and joint pain a person might be experiencing.

Cupping Therapy

It is an ancient therapy where the therapist will place cups on your skin a few times to create a suction. The treatment aims to help in increasing the blood flow, help inflammation, and deep tissue massages.

Dry needling

Dry needling is a procedure where acupuncture or a fine needle is inserted into skin & muscle. It is generally used to trigger hyperirritable points. It helps in reducing pain and restoring function.
