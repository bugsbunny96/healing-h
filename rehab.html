Post-Operative Rehab

Total Knee Replacement

Knee replacement surgery is performed to help relieve the pain of severely diseased knee joints. It is also widely known as knee arthroplasty (ARTH-row-plas-tee). In this surgery, the damaged bones & cartilage are cut away from your shinbone, thighbone, and kneecap, replacing them with an artificial joint. These joints are made of high-grade polymers, plastics, and metal alloys.

Total Hip Replacement

In total, Hip Replacement, also known as hip arthroplasty, the damaged cartilage & bone is removed plus replaced with prosthetic components. This surgery is suggested if your hip has been damaged by fracture, arthritis, and feeling pain while walking.

Bone Fracture Rehabilitation

Bone fractures, often known as shattered bones, are a common issue among millions of people. These excruciating wounds take time and are frequently brought on by sports injuries, auto accidents, or falls. To treat fractures, we have several treatments available.

Tendon Repair

A tendon graft is required if the tendon has been seriously damaged. A tendon from another area of the body or an artificial tendon is employed in this situation. The surgeon examines the area to see if there are any nerves and blood vessel injuries.
